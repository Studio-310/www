About Studio 310

Studio 310 is your trusted hair salon located in the heart of Boksburg. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional hair care services in a welcoming and professional environment.

Our experienced stylists are passionate about helping you look and feel your best. We stay current with the latest trends and techniques while maintaining the classic styles our clients love.

Using only premium products and advanced techniques, we ensure every client leaves feeling confident and beautiful. Visit us at our convenient location in Impala Park and experience the Studio 310 difference.
